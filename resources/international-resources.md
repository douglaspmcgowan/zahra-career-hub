# International Student & Worker Resources — Bay Area / Berkeley

## 1. UC Berkeley Resources

### International Students and Scholars Office (ISSO)
- **URL:** [internationaloffice.berkeley.edu](https://internationaloffice.berkeley.edu)
- Advises F-1 and J-1 students on immigration, employment authorization (CPT, OPT, Academic Training), compliance
- Runs OPT and STEM OPT workshops regularly during the academic year

### Berkeley Career Engagement
- **URL:** [career.berkeley.edu](https://career.berkeley.edu)
- Career counseling for international students — navigating work authorization timelines and employer sponsorship
- Hosts employer panels where visa-sponsoring companies recruit

### Cal Alumni Association / Berkeley Network
- **URL:** [alumni.berkeley.edu](https://alumni.berkeley.edu)
- Networking and mentorship programs open to current students and recent graduates
