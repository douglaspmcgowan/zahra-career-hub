## Key Visa Pathway: E-3 (Australian Specialty Worker)

The E-3 visa is exclusively for Australians and is Zahra's strongest immigration option:
- ~10,500 annual slots (rarely filled — no lottery needed)
- Requires a job offer in a "specialty occupation" (degree-required role)
- Renewable indefinitely
- Simpler and faster than H-1B
